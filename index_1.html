Why SquatCoach?

I started squatting with weights about a year ago. However, I never saw much gains. After a couple of form checks from people who know better, it turns out that the reason was simple: I wasn't squatting deep enough.

Frustrated with having someone to spot me every time, I decided to check if there were any apps that could monitor my squat progress. Much to my disappointment, there weren't any good ones - most apps are filled with unnecessary bullshit. So I built my own, no-bullshit app that does exactly what I want it to do.

Someone in the gym noticed my phone strapped to my thighs, and asked me what it does. After explaining, I found 5 other people who were also interested in having something that told them if they were squatting with good form or not, and the rest, as they say, is history.

Using SquatCoach, I deloaded back to just squatting the bar, and focused on learning proper form. I know that I was squatting deep enough when my phone started vibrating on my thigh. 2.5kg by 2.5kg, I now progressed to the point where I can squat my own body weight, without much wobbling in my knees, and with good depth.

As I progress more with my squat weights, I am also increasingly aware of butt-wink and other bad forms of squats, and I'll be actively developing SquatCoach to detect and provide actionable feedback on those.
