Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us. Here we would like to inform you about our policy for protecting your privacy with the use of the app. As a business, we DO collect information from you, and we do it in two ways: Information we get from your usage of this website, information from the usage of the app and information you give us.

The App

As far as the app goes (on both the iOS version as well as the Android version), we do not collect any information from the app. Nothing will be transmitted to any servers from the SquatCoach app. Do be aware that by using Google Play Store, you will be subject to Google's Privacy Policy and by using the App Store, you will be subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

On This Website

We collect data using Google Analytics on this website. We use it to analyze how users use this site, and make decisions to improve the website. Google Analytics uses cookies, which are transmitted to Google, which then compiles statistical reports based on user activity for SquatCoach

Information You Give Us

We do not require you to give us any information about yourself in order to use the app.
